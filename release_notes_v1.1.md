**Summary**
Schematic, coordination-level drawing package for a combined automotive & diesel training facility sized for 24 students (light-duty primary, heavy-duty secondary, EV integrated). This set is intended for planning, facilities review, and early MEP coordination (not construction/engineered documents).

**Package contents**

* Discipline-separated DXFs: ARCH_Plan.dxf, ELEC_Plan.dxf, PLUMB_Plan.dxf, MECH_Plan.dxf, EQUIP_Plan.dxf, FURN_Plan.dxf
* Layer/symbol legend: Drawing_Legend_and_Layer_Guide.pdf
* Executive summary: Executive_Summary_v1.1.pdf
* Distribution templates: Email_Draft_v1.1.txt, Email_Draft_Grant_v1.1.txt, Email_Draft_Perkins_v1.1.txt
* PNG previews (non-CAD review): /previews

**Key program counts (from master set)**

* Light-duty lifts: 12
* Heavy-duty lifts: 4
* EV chargers: 2
* Toolboxes: 12

**Notes / Disclaimer**
MEP drawings are placeholders for coordination only; not sized or stamped.
